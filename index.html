Welcome to Blair Space Club!

Blair Space Club is a student-led group based at Montgomery Blair High School in Silver Spring, MD. Our mission is to foster engineering and aerospace interest within the local community and beyond.

We actively participate in various projects, including The American Rocket Challenge (TARC), where our members design, build, and launch rockets. Additionally, we collaborate with other schools on ambitious projects such as designing and launching a CubeSat into space.

Join us for a range of exciting activities and events as we explore the wonders of space together!

Blair Space Club is fiscally sponsored by The Hack Foundation (d.b.a. Hack Club), a 501(c)(3) nonprofit (EIN: 81-2908499).
